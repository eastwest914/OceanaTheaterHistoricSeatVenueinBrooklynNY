About Oceana Theater

OceanaTheater is an ideal venue for concerts, theatrical performances, comedy shows, as well as corporate events, conferences, political meetings, and so on.

Now is a modern, excellently equipped 1326-seat venue located in the heart of South Brooklyn's immensely popular Brighton Beach Area, by the beautiful ocean beaches, near convenient transportation means (subway, easy driving access).

Our Historic Journey

1934

OceanaTheater Opens

The venue first opened its doors in 1934, becoming a cultural landmark in Brooklyn.

Later
Years

Atlantic Oceana Era

Known as the Atlantic Oceana, then the Millennium Theater and the Master Theater.

2022

Return to Historic Name

In 2022, the theater proudly returned to its historical name - Oceana Theater.
